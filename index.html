Who We Are

Anglican by roots.
RADICAL IN expression.

As the youth arm of an Anglican church, we honor the Anglican traditions that built us, while stepping into a bold, modern pursuit of God. When we gather, our praise is loud, our prayers are fervent.

Scripture

We obey and preach the Bible as the living, active Word of God. Every page is trustworthy, every command true, and we do not change it for comfort. Scripture is our standard, the foundation we stand on without exception.

Liturgy

We honor our ancient Anglican heritage with spoken responses, prayers, and sacramental rites. The liturgy shapes our worship rhythm, keeps our praises rooted in truth, and helps us engage God with reverence that is humble, disciplined, and alive and faithful.

Community

We build real relationships where honesty matters, accountability strengthens us, and love is practiced daily. In this community we carry one another's burdens, celebrate each other's victories, and grow together as brothers and sisters who are real, rooted, and committed.

Mission

Strength is youth's glory, so we build our spiritual house while we still have the muscle to lift the bricks. We honor God, lay deep foundations, and devote our strength to Him before age, struggle, or death make it impossible.

"For we are God's handiwork, created in Christ Jesus to do good works, which God prepared in advance for us to do."
— Ephesians 2:10
